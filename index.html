FAQ

Straight answers so you know what you’re buying.

"Do you guarantee #1 on Google or map-pack placement?"
No. Anyone promising guaranteed rankings is selling you a fantasy. I build the foundation and maintain consistency, but Google controls the rules.
"Is the website editable with an admin panel?"
The landing page is delivered as a static site with no admin panel. Maintenance covers minor edits within the existing layout.
"What do you need from me to start?"
Business name, services, service area, contact info, preferred photos, and any booking/contact preferences. If you’re missing items, I’ll tell you exactly what to gather.
"Can I bundle website + GBP?"
Yes. Website and GBP are separate services, but bundling often makes sense if you want a consistent message across your site and Google profile.
"Can we add...?"
Probably, but that might not be included in the base price.
"What’s the payment policy?"
50% upfront to begin. No refunds on deliverables. Timelines depend on timely client feedback and access needed for GBP.
