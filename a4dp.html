About All 4 Dirty Paws

A small, owner-operated grooming shop built on one principle: treat every pet like they’re yours.

We focus on a safe environment, gentle handling, and consistent results. That means we don’t rush the process, and we don’t overpromise. We’ll tell you what’s realistic for your pet’s coat, comfort, and maintenance routine.

Beyond grooming, we carry all-natural food and treats—because long-term pet wellness is more than a haircut.

FAQ

Clear answers—no guessing, no fluff.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We recommend calling to schedule. It ensures we can give your pet the time and attention they deserve.

How do you price grooming?

Pricing depends on size, coat type, coat condition (matting), and the time required. Call with your pet’s details and we’ll set expectations up front.

What if my pet is anxious?

Tell us when you call. We use a comfort-first approach and will recommend the best plan based on your pet’s needs.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Bring any notes about sensitivities, behavior, and coat history. Photo references are helpful if you have a specific style in mind.
